BEST VALUE TECHNOLOGY, INC.

BVTI is a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Business (SDVOB) that provides services to the United States Federal Government. BVTI has repeatedly impressed its clients with dedication to quality and application of creative problem solving. Our niche is information technology and program management supporting national security.

SECURITY ENGINEERING: HOW TO RELIABLY SECURE YOUR FACILITIES?

BVTI can lay plans for SCIF construction, taking into account risks of entry/exit, information technology, and external physical security; and design and construct the SCIF to industry standards. Whether designing a new building, with conference rooms, IT networks, and plumbing facilities; or secure construction accommodation within an existing structure; BVTI has the experience and knowledge to provide security plans to suit a variety of needs.
